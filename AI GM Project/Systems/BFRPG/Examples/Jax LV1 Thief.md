\[MECHANICAL PAYLOAD: Jax "The Rat"\]  
Below are the strict BFRPG mechanics for Jax. The Engine must enforce these rules.

**\*\*The Sneak Attack (Assassination)\*\***  
\* **\*\*Mechanic:\*\*** If Jax attacks an enemy from behind while unnoticed, he gains \+4 to his Melee Attack Roll: 1d20 \+ 5 (+0 STR, \+1 AB, \+4 Sneak) vs AC. If the attack hits, roll Damage (1d6) and multiply the final result by 2\.  
\* **\*\*Narrative Flavor:\*\*** Jax drops from the shadows or steps out from a blind spot, driving his shortsword directly into the exposed joints of his target's armor before they even know he's there.

**\*\*Shortbow (Ranged Skirmish)\*\***  
\* **\*\*Mechanic:\*\*** Ranged Attack Roll: 1d20 \+ 4 (+2 DEX, \+1 AB, \+1 Halfling Bonus) vs AC. Damage: 1d6.  
\* **\*\*Narrative Flavor:\*\*** Jax keeps his distance, nocking arrows with rapid precision and firing into the fray while Kaelen holds the frontline. 

**\*\*Thief Skills (The Specialist)\*\***  
\* **\*\*Mechanic:\*\*** When attempting to bypass a lock, trap, or sneak past guards, the GM must roll a d100 against Jax's skill percentages (e.g., Open Locks is 25%). If the d100 rolls equal to or lower than the target number, he succeeds.  
\* **\*\*Narrative Flavor:\*\*** A tense, sweat-inducing moment of clicking tumblers and taught wires as Jax uses his delicate tools to ensure the party doesn't get blown to pieces.
